## Goal

Create a 60 to 120 second founder-led walkthrough that introduces VoiceBar as a local-first Mac voice workflow tool for privacy-conscious Artificial Intelligence (AI) productivity.

## Audience

- Mac users who want local dictation, snippets, and text-to-speech.
- Builders who want human-controlled automation.
- Open-source contributors who care about privacy, governance, and review discipline.

## Narrative Position

VoiceBar is a practical proof-of-work by Live. It connects to the wider evidence-first governance philosophy behind Digital Dance AI (DDAI) and Evidary without making either organisation required to use VoiceBar.
